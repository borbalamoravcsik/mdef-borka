This question sounds challenging to me...

To involve ways in our education systems and in our every day life as a community or as a neighbourhood to raise a conscious generation who have voice and aware of the weight of their decisions. Since one third of four billion city dwellers are children, and by 2050, 70% of the youngest ones will live in cities according to UNICEF, we have make them aware that they are the ones who have to deal we the accelerating urbanisation and they have to be involved in creating an environment for out generations to be able to live together. Both stakeholders and experts need to have their voices heard. The MDEF creates the ideal conditions for this kind of dialog. How can we educate kids to be able to form their own future while involving decision makers in some common thinking and planning.
