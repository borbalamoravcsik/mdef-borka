Design Studio 02 - 3rd class

Network of co-responsibility. (Co-)designing for emergent futures in the present.

This week we had a really interesting talk with Sergio Uruena, who is a technologist and philosopher about how we shape society through innovation and how we must use responsibility when creating and using technologies.

It was always a very important topic to me, because mostly in my practice I was dealing with social design or services which influence huge amount of people. In the begging of the year I started doing research on ethical research and ethical design. Now that in these trimesters mostly my designs - the interventions are the frames of my research it is very interesting to connect the two of them and conduct ethical research with my participants through ethically designed interventions.

There was an important point in Sergio’s talk, I will take with me:
“Focus on the quality, inclusion and transparency of the relations.” - which makes me think more responsibly also about the ‘non-human agents’ of my research, everything that can be affected by it later after engaging with my human participants and everything that is also engaged at the same time, when my human participants.

Responsibility is a keyword in my research, because it is very clear when I am engaging with younger generations and citizens of their own environment, my alternative present is aiming for a more responsible alternative than the current one.
