Design intervention - Aprendizaje Emocionante

We studied in systems created by the needs of previous generations and led by them. Today's needs changed and the hierarchy in teaching and learning should change as well.
How can we change what we learn and how we learn it?
How can we create new ways of learning, by raising our voice and showing our needs? Also how can we be aware of those needs?

Our collective wants to explore the different ways of learning. As part of this exploration we want to collect all individual learning paths of people in Barcelona through a collective reflective experience.During this event, we will invite you to join us and draw the map of your learning journey. Since you were born until today, which important moments shaped you? Think of life events, people, places...
